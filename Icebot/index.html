Icebot has been released!

I am very pleased to say that Icebot has been released to the public. I have been working on this project daily for the last 5 weeks and I am very proud of what I accomplished. You can invite Icebot to your servers whenever you like. Thank you for using my bot!

Fun

Icebot has many different commands that you and your members can use for entertainment. Here is a brief list: Rock,Paper,Scissors, Coinflip, Covid19 Stats. If you don't see a command you would like, go ahead and contact me on Discord and I will probably add it :)

Moderation

At launch, Icebot has very basic moderation commands, allowing moderators and admins to purge up to 100 messages at a time sent in the last 2 weeks as well as having a basic kick and ban command. Icebot's moderation WILL be fleshed out more a little while after launch after seeing what server owners want the most. If you would like to be apart of this process join the official SyntaxisCS discord!

Leveling

Icebot is equipped with a leveling system that rivals mee6. Featuring a better leveling algorithm, and a more finely tuned amount of xp per message allows for your members to have more fun racing each other to the top of your server's leaderboard!
